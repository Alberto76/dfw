David Foster Wallace is an American author known for his novels, short stories, and essays. He has written for The New Yorker, Harper’s, and The Atlantic Monthly, and was in 2012 a finalist for the Pulitzer Prize in fiction. Wallace was raised in rural Illinois, the subject of several of his essays. Read More
